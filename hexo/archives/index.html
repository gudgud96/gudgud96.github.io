TAN HAO HAO

More about me?

Back in the old days...

I was raised in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, and I used to study in a Chinese Independent High School. I was once a trilingual public speaker, and I had also joined the Chinese Debate team for 6 years. Debate greatly shaped my way of thinking and personality, making me who I am today.

About coding...

I started coding after graduating from high school, when I decided to pursue my studies in Nanyang Technological University, Singapore as a Computer Science student. I like Math a lot, and coding is just like it - you crave for the satisfaction of problem solving!

...and music!

Music for me is something really special - it is the spice element that brings in vibe and soul into life. Band sessions with friends feel like a cool breeze amidst your hectic schedule. I'm working with my own piano covers, as well as a Mandopop band that I am in called SIGMA.

Where am I heading to?

Skills are being honed to serve one's true interest, and I think that AI is the best tool today that we should all equip ourselves with to realize great ideas. I am particularly interested in the field of AI creativity, to see how machine learning can be introduced to the field of music-making.
